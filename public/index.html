Thanks for your interest in our project!

You are eligible to participate in the study if you:
  • are 18+ years old,
  • use Google Chrome on regular basis,
  • never used any of the following browser extensions: Disconnect, Ghostery, or any kind of ad blockers,
  • allow third-party cookies in your browser,
  • have not participated in any of our previous user studies, except the one with the Android app.

During the study we will ask you to download our Chrome extension and keep it on for a week. After that, you will be rewarded a £5 Amazon gift voucher for your participation.
